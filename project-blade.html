Airfoil Blade Design

For my class MAE 4272, we designed an airfoil optimized for power at 1000 RPM. Given our Weibull Distribution, we calculated a design wind speed of 5.05 m/s.

To optimize power, we designed for a constant angle of attack of about 9.75 degrees, twisting the pitch angle to maintain efficiency throughout the blade span.

Weibull Distribution and Wind Analysis

Experimental results showed the optimal rotation rate was 1054 RPM. Although the power generation was an order of magnitude less than expected due to modeling assumptions, the curves aligned with our predicted peaks.
