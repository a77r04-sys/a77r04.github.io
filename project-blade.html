Airfoil Blade Design

Optimization

Given our Weibull Distribution, we calculated which wind speed to design for, which was determined to be 5.05 m/s. To optimize power, we designed for a constant angle of attack of about 9.75 degrees, twisting the pitch angle to maintain this throughout the blade length.

Technologies Used: MATLAB, Autodesk Fusion

Experimental Results

Our results showed that our airfoils did generate the most power near 1000 RPM; however, the power generation was an order of magnitude less than expected. My work involved developing the code and main design choices in determining geometry.
