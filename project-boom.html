Boom Deployment Mechanism

As a research assistant for Cornell Space Structures Lab, I am working on a boom deployment mechanism that can deploy an ultra lightweight boom from a CubeSat without the boom buckling or blossoming.

High-speed capture of successful boom deployment.

Research & Design

To do this, I have designed a low-friction membrane that surrounds the boom at every region, except when the boom exits the CubeSat and holds it tight through springs that are attempting to revert back to their original length.

First Prototype: This prototype was longer and used 4 sets of springs.
New Prototype: This prototype only uses two sets of springs, is currently not yet finished but we hope for success with the membrane successfully holding in over 75 percent of the boom.
