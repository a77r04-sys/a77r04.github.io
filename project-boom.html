Boom Deployment Mechanism

Skills Mechanical Design Stepper Motors Space Systems

As a research assistant for Cornell Space Structures Law, I worked on a boom deployment that could deploy an ultra-lightweight boom from a CubeSat without the boom buckling or blossoming. To do this, I designed two rollers that rotated together with high friction to pull out the boom with a constant tension. The rollers were coupled together, rotated by one stepper motor, which could be set to start and stop to bend the boom into the desired shape at the exit of the deployment mechanism.
