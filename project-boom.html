Boom Deployment Mechanism

Mechanism DesignStepper MotorsPrototypingSpace Systems

As a research assistant for Cornell Space Structures Lab, I am working on a boom deployment mechanism that can deploy an ultra lightweight boom from a CubeSat without the boom buckling or blossoming.

To do this, I have designed two rollers that rotate together in with high friction to pull out the boom with a constant tension.

Mechanism with boom in fully coiled configuration

Control & Actuation

The rollers are coupled together and rotated by one stepper motor, which can be set to start and stop to bend the boom into the desired shape at the exit of the deployment mechanism.
