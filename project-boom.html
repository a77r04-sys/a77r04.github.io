Boom Deployment Mechanism

Skills Mechanical Design Stepper Motors Space Systems

As a research assistant for the Cornell Space Structures Lab, I am developing a deployment mechanism that releases an ultra-lightweight boom from a CubeSat without buckling or blossoming.

My design uses two high-friction rollers coupled together and driven by a single stepper motor — pulling the boom out under constant tension. By modulating start/stop of the stepper, the boom can be bent into the desired shape as it exits the housing.
