Boom Deployment Mechanism

The Mechanism

Working at the Cornell Space Structures Lab, I designed a mechanism to deploy ultra lightweight booms without buckling or blossoming. To do this, I designed two rollers that rotate together with high friction to pull out the boom with constant tension.

Boom fully coiled configuration

Control

The rollers are coupled together and rotated by one stepper motor, which can be set to start and stop to bend the boom into desired shapes at the exit of the deployment mechanism.
