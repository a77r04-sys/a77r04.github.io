Automatic Soup Stirrer

As a part of MAE 2250 (Mechanical Synthesis), I worked on a semester-long group project to build a consumer-ready project. We built an automatic soup stirrer that would stir soup for an individual who does not have the time to constantly monitor the pot.

CAD & Validation

After working through many potential designs as a team and comparing the tests of multiple prototypes, we decided on a final design. I designed and created in CAD the final design almost entirely myself. We validated the concept through fluid testing, where the device successfully stirred water and high-viscosity stews.
