Automatic Soup Stirrer

The Concept

As a part of MAE 2250 (Mechanical Synthesis), I worked on a semester-long group project to build a consumer-ready project. We built an automatic soup stirrer that would stir soup for an individual who does not have the time.

Early low-cost physical prototype

Final Execution

After working through many potential designs as a team, we decided on a final design. I designed and created in CAD the final design almost entirely myself.

Validation: Successfully stirring water in final tests
