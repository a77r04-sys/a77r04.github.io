Automatic Soup Stirrer

Designed as part of MAE 2250 (Mechanical Synthesis). A consumer-ready device built to automate the stirring process for home cooks.

Design Process

Our goal was to build a consumer-ready product for individuals who do not have the time to constantly monitor simmering soups. After comparing multiple prototypes as a team, we settled on a gantry-style design.

I was responsible for the final CAD design (Autodesk Fusion), handling the integration of the motor housing, stirring paddle, and pot-clamping mechanism.

Final design rendered in Autodesk Fusion

Prototyping & Testing

We validated the design through physical testing, ensuring the device could maintain consistent stirring speed in fluids of varying viscosity (from water to thick stews).
