Mechanical Design Iteration 02

Final Prototype

Skills Autodesk Inventor 3D Printing

The final prototype uses a linear actuator and springs to drive a gripping mechanism that flexes farther inward — clamping more securely on rocks. TPU pads conform to rugged edges. A camera and laser assist the controls team. The self-actuating screwdriver was removed in this iteration to focus on grip reliability.
