Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering Senior at Cornell University.

I am the lead of the arm sub-team of the Cornell Mars Rover project team, working to build a rover to compete in the URC. This past summer, I was a Deployable Technologies Intern at Physical Sciences Inc., designing a rescue litter gripper, testing low-lubricity fluid pumps, and developing a mobile folding bridge. I have performed research for the Cornell ASTRA Lab on electrospray propellants and the Space Structures Lab on boom deployment mechanisms.
