End Effector - First Prototype

Mechanism

The first prototype of the end effector utilized a 4 linkage system and a DC motor. The end effector also had a camera and self-actuating screwdriver.

Problems encountered with this prototype mostly involved too many points of failure in the 3D prints and too much weight.
