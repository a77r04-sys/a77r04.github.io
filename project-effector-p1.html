First Prototype

Skills Milling Autodesk Inventor

The first prototype utilized a 4-linkage system and a DC motor, with an onboard camera and a self-actuating screwdriver. I manufactured the motor mount from Aluminum 6062 on a mill. The main pain points were too many failure points in the 3D-printed components and excess weight — both addressed in the final design.
