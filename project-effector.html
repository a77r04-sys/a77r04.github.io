End Effector

Skills CAD Autodesk Inventor Machining

As part of the Cornell Mars Rover project team, I designed and built an end effector for the rover's arm — able to pick up irregular rocks, carry up to 5 kg, and complete dexterous tasks: typing, screwdriving, joystick manipulation, and switch flipping.

01Prototype

First Prototype

4-linkage system, DC motor, integrated camera and self-actuating screwdriver.
02Final design

Final Prototype

6-linkage gripper with spring-loaded TPU pads, linear actuator, camera and laser.
