Structure of Robotic Manipulator

In my junior year, I designed the structure of the robotic manipulator for the Cornell Mars Rover Project Team. I redesigned the locations of the motors, moving one of the motors to the elbow of the manipulator, in order to reduce the moment created on the base of the robotic manipulator. I took the lead in assembling, wiring, and testing the arm.

Analysis & Optimization

Finite Element Analysis (FEA) was performed on critical joints to ensure the manipulator could sustain a $5$ kg load at full extension without structural failure. This led to a significant reduction in structural weight while maintaining high stiffness.
