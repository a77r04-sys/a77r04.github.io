Robotic Manipulator Structure

I am the lead of the arm sub-team of the Cornell Mars Rover project team, working to build a rover to compete in the University Rover Competition (URC).

Demonstration of the 6-DOF manipulator in motion.

Technical Overview

The robotic manipulator is a custom system designed to meet the rigorous demands of the URC. It is engineered to carry up to 5kg while maintaining the precision required for dexterous tasks. My role involves overseeing the arm sub-team throughout the design, CAD, and manufacturing phases to ensure full mechanical integration with the rover chassis.

Final structural assembly of the robotic manipulator.
