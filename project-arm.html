Structure of Robotic
Manipulator

In my junior year, I designed the structure of the robotic manipulator for the Cornell Mars Rover Project Team. I redesigned the locations of the motors, moving one of the motors to the elbow of the manipulator, in order to reduce the moment created on the base of the robotic manipulator.

My Final Design Review presentation can be found at: FDR Link

Mobility and joint articulation test

I took the lead in assembling, wiring, and testing the arm. Finite Element Analysis (FEA) was performed on critical joints to ensure the manipulator could sustain the required 5kg load at full extension without structural deflection.
