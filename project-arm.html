Structure of Robotic Manipulator

Skills: CADANSYS FEADMFASystems Integration

In my junior year, I designed the structure of the robotic manipulator for the Cornell Mars Rover Project Team. I redesigned the locations of the motors, moving one of the motors to the elbow of the manipulator, in order to reduce the moment created on the base of the robotic manipulator. I took the lead in assembling, wiring, and testing the arm. My Final Design Review presentation can be found at: FDR Link.
